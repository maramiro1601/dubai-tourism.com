About The museum Of the Future

The building is an architectural and engineering marvel.

It confidently straddles the past and the future, applying advanced technology to traditional artforms.

The building ‘speaks Arabic’: its facade is a canvas for the poetry of His Highness Sheikh Mohammed Bin Rashid Al Maktoum rendered in the calligraphy of Mattar bin Lahej.

Explore the jewel of Jumeirah and enjoy its private beach.

The building’s form is futuristic: it opens a new path away from the highrise towers that dominate skylines everywhere.

Its form is symbolic: The circular building represents humanity; the green mound it sits atop represents the earth; the void represents the unknown future.

The Museum of The Future shows the visitors how the world, especially Dubai, will be 50 years from now.

A visit to this museum doesn't take you to the past, as museums usually do.
